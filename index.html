For the orphanges, For the volunteers, For the youth

There are three reasons to support AKAP. (1) AKAP is a youth-led organization meant to assist multiple orphanages in Davao City with supplies and assistance. The organization holds merch drops, donation boxes, and other activities for charitable people to donate for these orphanges. (2) This organization is entirely youth-led with volunteers working in design, social media, logistics, management, and etc. It is perfect to donate your talents in kindness for these children. (3) The project empowers the youth. It empowers Philippine Science High School to serve their nation and fellow citizens and builds a better future for the world one kaychain, one social media post, and one donation at a time.
